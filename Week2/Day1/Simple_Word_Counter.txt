Pseudo-code Problem - Simple Word Counter

Start
1. Input: paragraph of text.
2. Split text into words.
3. Normalize words to lowercase.
4. Remove punctuation.
5. Use Map<Word, Count> to store frequency.
6. Use Stream to process and count.
7. Sort words by frequency and display.
8. Example:
   Input: "The news today is about the rise in technology trends."
   Output: the(2), news(1), today(1), is(1), about(1), rise(1), in(1), technology(1), trends(1)
End
